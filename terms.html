Terms And Conditions

  • Students must abide by the rules and regulations of Packapeer.

  • Students are expected to keep the utmost level of discipline, while they are in the center premises.

  • A student should not involve him/herself in disruption/disturbance to teaching process.

  • Assignments are needed to be submitted on due date.

  • The admission will be on basis of Screening test result.

  • Payment must be received prior to start of each quadmester.

  • Fee once paid is not refundable under any circumstances.

  • The student is expected to attend all the classes and tests to get the full benefit of the program and sustain his eligibility for program benefits.

  • As per our rules 80% attendance is mandatory to appear in quadmester end examination.

  • After each quadmester there will be an examination to test the knowledge and ability of the student.

  • Every student should score 80% in each quadmester end examination to achieve the goal of 1 lakh range monthly salary within 3 years from course completion.

  • If the student scored 60% in each quadmester end examination, we will only assure with salary goal of INR 50,000 per month within three years from course completion.

  • If the student scored 50% marks in each quadmester end examination we will only assure with guarantee placement without salary specification.

  • It is mandatory for students to attend Dev Pro.

  • Students should satisfy at least 80% conditions set by us during Dev Pro to achieve one lakh range salary goal.

  • Students after achieving their job should submit their bank statement, salary certificate and offer letter as a proof of salary received during Dev Pro.

  • Packapeer reserves the right to modify the course syllabus.
