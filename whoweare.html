Who We Are

Only real Engineer can unravel true potential of an Engineer !!!!


We are a pool of engineers with years of experience in IT industry in top companies. During the last decade, founders of Packapeer have been providing training activities for different organisations.


In 2011 founders of Packapeer started Crazy Amigos, a non profit organisation focusing on providing quality technical education throughout Kerala. In between founders of Packapeer were able to build two startups and so have real industry and entrepreneurial knowledge.


Later on started a training division in Kochi named Hot Shot Mentors which successfully hosted a one year program where 12 students from different degree programs were trained and placed in software companies as highly paid employees. All these activities were based on Kochi.


In recent past Crazy amigos expanded community activities to Calicut. Recently Crazy Amigos have been renamed to Ping Foundation and hosted lot of events and workshops.
